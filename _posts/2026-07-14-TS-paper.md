Svetlana's new paper characterizing a zebrafish knock-in model with the Timothy syndrome mutation is <a href = "https://www.nature.com/articles/s42003-026-10657-3">available</a>. Based on the previously reported sensitiviy of the Cav1.2 channel to temperature, this paper raises the intriguing possibility that fever might be a modifiable risk for patients with Timothy syndrome.
